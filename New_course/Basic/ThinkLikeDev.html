Solving problems !

In 4 steps ! At first, we need to stay calm, slow down and don't jump into problems without solutions. Take logical and rational approach

  • Do I 100% understand the problem ? What are the goals, objectives ?I have to understand everything
  • Divide and conquer : break big problems into a lot of sub/smaller problems. To do that, I need previous problem solve
  • Make a lot of research and do not hesitate to find solutions to our problems.
  • Make pseudo-code to us to understand before giving it to machine language.
